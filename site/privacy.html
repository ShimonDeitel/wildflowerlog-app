Privacy Policy

Wildflower Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information.

The app uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases; purchase processing is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's own privacy policy.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any network tracking.
